Download Crow corpus for offline use

Please find the downloadable version of the Crow corpus below. As a reminder from the training:

  • Do not share this with anyone, including other members of your research team. All team members should go through the training before using the corpus.
  • Do not store the corpus on a portable device (including laptop or flash drive). Make sure to use password protected cloud or institutional servers to store the data.
  • Please cite the corpus when using it in any presentations or publications (Staples, S., & Dilger, B. (2018-). Corpus and repository of writing [Learner corpus articulated with repository]. Available at https://crow.corporaproject.org)
  • Please notify us of any issues you encounter with the offline corpus, particularly any identifiable information you might find: https://crow.corporaproject.org/problems
In order to download the corpus for offline use, you will need to Request access.
